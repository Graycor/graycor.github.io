Questions leadership will ask

Is it reliable enough to run our church on?
Yes. Full weekly backups kept three ways, an audit trail on sensitive actions, and role-based access so the right people see the right things. It’s built to be depended on, not demoed and forgotten.
Who owns our data?
You do. Full stop. Export any time, and backups are downloadable — your information never gets held hostage.
Is it safe to put our members’ details in?
That’s the bar it’s built to. Hardened security headers, mandatory two-factor for volunteers, role-based access, an audit trail, and personal data kept off the public surface — designed to be safely hosted on the open internet.
Do we need an IT team?
No. It’s built to be run by volunteers, with a built-in knowledge base that documents every feature in plain language.
Is it really Christian software, or just software with a fish on it?
Built by a church, shaped by real ministry. Discipleship, safe ministry and Sunday mornings are first-class features, not bolt-ons.
How much does it cost?
Simple annual pricing, scaled to your church rather than your postcode. Small-church and plant pricing available — just ask.
Can it do [that specific thing]?
Probably — or it’s on the list. Tell sales what you need and we’ll be honest about where it’s at.
